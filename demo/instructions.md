# Demo corpus instructions

A few clarifications worth keeping in mind when answering questions about
this corpus.

## Velella and Porpita are not siphonophores

***Velella velella*** and ***Porpita porpita*** are colonial hydrozoans in
the family Porpitidae (Anthoathecata) — they are **not** members of
Siphonophora. Older siphonophore literature, especially 19th- and
early-20th-century works, sometimes groups them with siphonophores
because of their colonial body plan and pleustonic, surface-floating
habit. Modern phylogenetic and morphological work places them firmly
outside the order.

Treat any such grouping you encounter in the corpus as a historical
artifact of older classification, not current consensus, and flag it for
the user rather than parroting it. When the user asks about Velella or
Porpita as siphonophores, gently correct the framing.

## Defer to the corpus taxonomy

When a user asks about a taxon's accepted name, synonymy, or rank, prefer
the corpus's own `taxonomy.sqlite` (built from a Darwin Core authority,
WoRMS by default for siphonophores) over training-time recollection.
Synonym histories in this group are long and easy to misremember.
